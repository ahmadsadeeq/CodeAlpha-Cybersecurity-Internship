🛡️ Phishing Awareness Training

Learn how to recognize phishing emails, fake websites, and social engineering attacks.

1. What is Phishing?

Phishing is a cyberattack where attackers impersonate trusted organizations to steal credentials, financial information, or sensitive data.

  • Email Phishing
  • Spear Phishing
  • Smishing (SMS)
  • Vishing (Voice Calls)

2. Recognizing Phishing Emails

From: support@paypa1-security.com
Subject: URGENT: Verify Your Account

Your account will be suspended in 24 hours. Click below immediately.

Warning Signs

  • Suspicious sender address
  • Urgent language
  • Unexpected links or attachments
  • Requests for passwords

3. Spotting Fake Websites

Always inspect the URL carefully.

LegitimateFake
https://paypal.comhttps://paypa1-security.com
  • Check HTTPS
  • Watch for misspellings
  • Verify domain ownership

4. Social Engineering Tactics

😨 Fear
⏰ Urgency
👔 Authority
🎁 Rewards

Attackers manipulate emotions to make victims act without thinking.

5. Best Practices

  • Enable Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)
  • Use strong passwords
  • Verify URLs before clicking
  • Keep software updated
  • Report suspicious emails

6. Real-World Example

An employee received a fake Microsoft login email and entered credentials into a fraudulent site. Attackers gained access and stole company data.

Lesson: Verify before you trust.
